Instructions


General

Falsehoods is a game where you either try to trick your opponent or guess the right answer depending on your role. At the start of a round, you will have a host and players. A question is provided and the players will then have a chance to submit a potential answer to that question. You can try to trick the host or make them laugh, either will earn you points. After answers are submitted, the host will make their selections and points will be provided accordingly. A new round may then begin.


Host

As host, you will receive a game code that you can send to other players to join your game. Once everyone has joined your lobby, you may begin your game.

At the start of the game, a question is presented. As host, you will wait for the other players to submit potential answers to the question. After those answers are submitted, the players answers as well as the correct answer will be revealed. It is then your task to select the correct answer to the provided question. You may also select an answer that you find funny or enjoyable as well.

After selecting your answer, the true answer will be revealed, along with who provided which fake answer. Points will be awarded as described below. You then have the option to begin another round or leave the game.


Player

As a player, you can select to join a game. A host will have to provide you with a game code that you can then use to enter the host's game. Once you've joined the game, a question will be provided and you will have a chance to submit an answer. Either try to trick the host into choosing your answer, or try to go for Funny Points and make them laugh. After you submit your answer, wait for the host to make a decision. Points will be awarded as described below. The host may then start another round or leave the game.


Points

As host, you will receive points for guessing the correct answer from amongst the false ones. You may also choose a "best" answer to award points to. One point is earned for each correct guess.

As a player, you will receive points for tricking the host or for having your answer chosen as the "best" answer. One point is earned for each.
